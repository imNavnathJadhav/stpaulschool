Directors's Message

"St. Paul's School strives to promote intellectual, emotional, spiritual, moral and physical development of the child. The School makes every effort on a regular basis to adopt and update its curricular and co-curricular activities to make education relevant and learning experience challenging and enjoyable for the students. The value based St. Paul's education, self discipline and high academic standard maintained by the school help the students face the challenges of life with courage, confidence and conviction."

- Rev. Dr. Valerian Fernandes
(M.A., B.Ed., Ph.D.)

St. Paul's School

St. Paul's School is an English Medium School founded by Rev. Fr. (Dr.) Valerian Fernandes in the year 1987 and it is run by St.Francis De Sales Education Society Aurangabad, located at Mudhalwadi, Paithan in Aurangabad district of Maharashtra, India. It has a Pre-Primary section, namely, Nursery, K.G. and Preparatory, Primary and High School. It is affiliated with Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education (MSBSHSE Board, Aurangabad).

Ajay Sarode Says...

I am from Mumbai and student of St Paul for 12 Year, Indeed I completed my entire schooling in St Paul. Today, I am a Digital Marketer in Firm at Mumbai.

It was my boarding experience that made me a better human today and I get recognization for the values and discipline that I have inculcated within me from the years of Learning.

Teachers we had in my time were Sister Florence, Bangar Sir, Thote Sir, Sucheta Maam and I am particularly grateful to Father Valerian. He was a pioneer of this school. Every brick of St Paul School has undergone through his blessing and grace and I consider I am one of that brick.

The year on the boarding school just really changed me completely as a person in all possible ways. During the year I grew into a much more mature and reflected person.
