Silver Jubilee of St. Paul’s English Medium High School at Paithan celebrated

by Admin

Paithan, 27 February 2011 : The Silver Jubilee of St. Paul’s High School at the historical town of Paithan in the Aurangabad District was celebrated on Saturday, 26th February 2011 with the solemn thanksgiving Mass at 11 am with Most Rev. Dr. Edwin Colaco, the Bishop of the Aurangabad Diocese as the chief celebrant and a grand function in the evening at 5.30 pm with Mr. Rajendra Darda, Minister of Education, Government of Maharashtra as the chief guest and attended by a number of dignitaries.

  The Thanksgiving Mass was offered by Most Rev. Dr. Edwin Colaco as the chief guest along with Rev. Dr. Valerian Fernandes and other priests from the Aurangabad Diocese. The Bishop and priests were welcomed in a traditional manner. A large number of religious sisters, local people and relatives of Rev. Dr. Valerian Fernandes from Mumbai, Kuwait, Kundapur, Udupi and Bangalore and Fr. Louis D’Sa-parish priest of Mudarangadi and a cousin of Rev. Dr. Valerian Fernandes attended the Thanksgiving Mass as well as the evening function. In his homily, Dr. Edwin Colaco highlighted the contribution of Rev. Dr. Valerian Fernandes in establishing St. Paul’s Church and St. Paul’s English Medium School at Paithan and the progress that has been made by the school during the last twenty-five years. Following the Mass the Bishop and Dr. Valerian Fernandes were honoured by the parishioners. A community meal was organized for all the participants after the end of the Thanksgiving Mass and the stage programme.

  The grand Silver Jubilee function in the evening was presided over by Dr. Edwin Colaco. Mr. Rajendra Darda, Minister of Education, Government of Maharashtra was the chief guest of the function. Other guests of honour included: Former Minister-Anil Patel, MLC from Aurangabad-M.M. Shaikh, Ex-MLA-Bhausaheb Thorat, Retired Deputy Superintendent of Police and presently the vice chairman of the Minority Cell of the Maharashtra State-James Ambildhage, Director of Sant Eknath Sugar Factory-K.P. Zhargad Mama and other local dignitaries were present for the programme.

  Following the ceremonial lighting of the lamp, Mr. Rajendra Darda inaugurated the Silver Jubilee Memorial Block with class rooms and the stage which was blessed in the morning by Dr. Edwin Colaco. The Silver Jubilee Souvenir was also released by Mr. Rajendra Darda. The minister and other dignitaries were felicitated by Dr. Valerian Fernandes. KP Zhargad Mama honoured Dr. Valerian Fernandes by tying the traditional Maharashtrian headgear ‘peta’.

  In his address, Mr. Rajendra Darda complimented Dr. Valerian Fernandes on the occasion of the Silver Jubilee of St. Paul’s English Medium High School and congratulated the staff and students for their efforts in making the school a model not only in Paithan but in the whole of the Aurangabad District. He also spoke about the government policy on education and the need for the improvement of the training of the teachers in order to enable them in imparting quality education. He underlined the fact that citizens should help the government in identifying the children who are not attending schools so that provision can be made to enrol such students in the schools. Other guests of honour also spoke on this occasion and greatly appreciated the quality education imparted at the school and the vision of Dr. Valerian Fernandes and the dedicated work of the headmistress and the teachers.

  The students of the school right from Lower KG to 10th standard presented variety entertainment programme in an excellent manner that regaled the guests and the audience who had gathered in large numbers.

  On the previous day, Friday, 25th February 2011, prize distribution programme was organised at 10.30 am. Nearly 200 students from all classes who had shown their excellence in various curricular and co-curricular activities and competitions were given prizes by the guests-James Ambildhage, K.P. Zhargad Mama, J.C. Francis, Fr. Louis D’Sa, Dr. Valerian Fernandes and Sr. Rochana Fernandes (CSST)-Headmistress of the school.

  St. Paul’s English Medium School that was founded 25 years ago by Rev. Dr. Valerian Fernandes, who hails from Halehalve (Pius Nagar) near Kundapur is the first English medium school in that region that has been responsible in providing quality education through English medium that has enabled a number of students in that region which has been considered as a backward area to have access to higher education and even some of the students from the school have gone abroad for specialized education. During the last 13 batches of SSC, 9 batches secured 100 per cent results and remaining 4 batches 98 per cent.
